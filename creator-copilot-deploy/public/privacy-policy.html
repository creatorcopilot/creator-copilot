Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Creator Copilot ("we," "us," or "our") operates creatorcopilot.org. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, phone number, business name, and content strategy information submitted through our intake form and demographic builder.

We also collect payment information processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your full credit card details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to deliver our service — including generating and sending daily content scripts via SMS, sending weekly brand deal emails, and providing niche intelligence reports.

Your phone number is used exclusively to send you content scripts and service notifications you signed up to receive. We will never use it for marketing unrelated to your Creator Copilot subscription.

SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number and completing our intake form, you consent to receive up to 7 SMS messages per week from Creator Copilot containing your daily content scripts and service notifications. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for help.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share information only with service providers necessary to operate our service, including Twilio (SMS delivery), Resend (email delivery), Stripe (payment processing), and Supabase (data storage). Each of these providers is bound by their own privacy policies and data protection agreements.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Your demographic profile is retained after cancellation so it can be used if you resubscribe. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing marketing@creatorcopilot.org.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored securely.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at marketing@creatorcopilot.org.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at marketing@creatorcopilot.org or visit creatorcopilot.org.
